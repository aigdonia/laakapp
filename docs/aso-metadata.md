## Description (4000 chars max)

> First 3 lines are visible before "more" tap — they must hook immediately.

```
Laak is your private investment companion — an investor friend that helps you understand what you own, how it's doing, and what to look at next.

Unlike basic screeners that give you pass/fail labels, Laak gives you real financial intelligence: AI-powered deep analysis, portfolio health scoring, and plain-language explanations of what matters. And yes — it filters for Sharia compliance too.

Everything stays on your device. No account required. No broker linking. No data harvesting.

UNDERSTAND YOUR INVESTMENTS
Run AI-powered deep analysis on any stock. Get plain-language breakdowns of financial health, revenue quality, debt structure, and sector positioning — not just a compliance label.

TRACK YOUR PORTFOLIO
See your holdings in one calm, visual dashboard. Allocation pie charts, gain/loss tracking, and a portfolio health score (0-100) that tells you where you stand at a glance.

SCREEN FOR COMPLIANCE
Sharia screening based on AAOIFI methodology with transparent ratios. See exactly why a stock passes or fails — debt-to-market-cap, revenue breakdown, purification amounts. No black boxes.

GUARD YOUR PRIVACY
Your portfolio data never leaves your device. No email required. No broker logins. Biometric lock keeps your finances private. Optional cloud backup only if you choose.

WORKS OFFLINE
Full portfolio tracking works without internet. Prices sync when you're online, cached for when you're not. Designed for real-world connectivity.

PAY ONLY FOR WHAT YOU USE
No forced subscriptions. Buy credits when you need deep analysis. Average users spend a fraction of what competing apps charge monthly. Privacy and portfolio tracking are always free.

BUILT FOR REAL INVESTORS
- AI stock deep-dives with financial reasoning
- Portfolio health gauge with diversification scoring
- Purification calculator (per-share amounts)
- Zakat estimation tools
- Multi-currency support
- Dark mode and biometric lock
- Available in English and Arabic

Laak covers US, Egyptian (EGX), and Saudi (Tadawul) markets with deep analysis. More markets coming based on demand.

Privacy policy: https://laak.app/privacy
Support: laak@olanai.tech
```
