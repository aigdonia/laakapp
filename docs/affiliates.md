# Egyptian Savings & Investment Platforms — Affiliate Partnership Map

> Last updated: March 2026
>
> Research scope: All platforms operating in Egypt that allow retail users to save, invest, or grow money — potential affiliate revenue partners for the Halal Wealth Visualizer.

---

## How This Fits Our App

Our app is a **visualizer** — it never touches money. When users discover portfolio needs (non-compliant holdings, no diversification, no gold exposure, want to start investing), the natural next question is "where do I act on this?" These platforms answer that question. We earn CPA/referral commissions for connecting the dots.

## Key Insights

1. **Most platforms have NOT publicly launched affiliate programs.** First-mover opportunity to propose partnerships before competitors lock them in.

2. **Thndr is the single most important partner** — 82% of new EGX retail investors go through them, they already have a referral program, and they offer Sharia-compliant funds.

3. **The gold space is fragmented** (mngm, Sabika, Dahab Masr, Gold Era, funds). Multiple partnership opportunities with different commission structures.

4. **Fractional real estate platforms (Nawy Shares, Farida, SAFE) are in aggressive growth mode** — highest likelihood of accepting affiliate partnerships because they need users now.

5. **Bokra is the strongest Sharia-first pure play** in Egypt and should be a priority — their identity aligns perfectly with ours.

6. **PALM is complementary, not competitive.** They manage money; we visualize it. A PALM user still needs our app to see their *full* portfolio across all platforms.

7. **Sabika's Saudi expansion plan** aligns with our Phase 2 — potential to build a cross-market affiliate relationship early.
